Help Needed to Map Triskari Prime

Exploration of Triskari Prime, out in the Vast, has begun. There is evidence of past civilization, but no evidence that any sentient life remains on the planet. The Kisharan xeno-druids are hiring contractors to map out an extensive system of tunnels found under the planet and to find out what happened the environmental monitoring system in the northern reaches of the planet.
